2. Book a group tour. Join an escorted group tour with a fixed departure date.

3. Plan and book a trip yourself.

Custom Tours

Escorted Group Tours
A group tour offers the advantage of convenience at a great value. The schedule is fixed, the itinerary is pre-planned, and a dedicated tour director travels with the group throughout the trip. A group tour can be 30 - 50% less expensive than an equivalent customized tour. Out of the many hundreds of group tours, how do you select the right one for you? Our travel agency division, Marageti, has sales agents who specialize in helping travelers weigh the pros and cons of each group tour. We give you personalized recommendations to find the best tour for you.

Ninh binh boasts its variety of majestic and natural landscape symbolizing the hidden beauty of northern rural area. In general, tourist can travel to Ninh Binh province at any time of the year. However, there are some best time in which you can explore the most magnificent charm of this land.

April and May : the best time to appreciate the ripen rice fields and the early harvest time

June: If you like take photo some lotus of Vietnam, this is a perfect time for you come to Ninh Binh

September and October – the package holidays to Vietnam take that takes place during September and October makes it possible to appreciate the wet rice harvest time. Any travelers that book a boat tour along the Bich Dong River can get to explore the Trang An Landscape Complex – Hoa Lu Ancient Capital – Tam Coc while being surrounded by the wonderful scenery of yellow rice fields.
The distance from Hanoi to Ninh Binh is about 85 kilometers.
It is not far distance, so that there, more option for you to get from Hanoi to Ninh Binh, you can suitable decide  with your budget.
