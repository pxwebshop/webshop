Luang Prabang, Laos has so many incredible thing to see and do - You will have a chance to discover the scenery beauty of nature and learning about culture and architecte of temples.  If you come to Laos don’t miss to visit top attractions in here. 

  1. Mount Phousi 

From Mount Phousi, you can see all most panorama of Luang Prabang, specially , it will great for sightseeing the wonderful sunrise if you can climb the more than 300 step to summit where you will find a temple

  1. Royal Temple Museum 

This is old government house where leader of Lao countries take over  and deciced all most work , but now it is a museum for traveler in world can go here to  find more information of Prabang Buddha statute that dates back 14h century

  1. What Xieng Thong.

Wat Xieng thong was built under the rule of King, and after that it change to royal temple under the patronage of the royal family

Wat Xieng Thong is one of the most temple in the UNESCO world heritage portion of Luang Prabang, this is the proof of cultural  Laos in decada ,

You can sightseeing some famous temple such as: Wat Mai, Wat A ham… 

  1. Kwang Si Falls

when Travelers coming to Luang Prabang, they  can’t forget to visit to Kuang Si Falls - the biggest and wonderful waterfall in Laos, Swimming the the most attractive activities in here. 

  1. Night Markets

it will be open from 5 pm until around 10 pm every nights of week. Coming here, you will have a chance to taste some delicious food in Laos, discover street food like fried mushroom and seaweed…. you will can choice some souvenir in night market. 

  1. Pak Ou Caves 

Near Pak Ou the Tham Ting and the Tham Theung are caves overlooking the Mekong River 25km to the north of Luang Prabang Laos. The cave are noted for their miniature Buddha sculptures. Hundreds of very small and mostly damaged wooden Buddhist figures are laid out over the wall shelves. They take many different positions, including meditation, teaching, peace, rain, and reclining (nirvana)

Luang Prabang have tropical climate same many southeast Asian countries, with temperatures ranging between 15c - 38c . The rainy season in Luang Prabang is from June to September, and the dry season begin from  between October in year to May year latter.

You can travel to Luang Prabang all of the time in the year but the best time for travelin in Luang Prabang is from November to March. The weather very comfortable with average temperatures.
