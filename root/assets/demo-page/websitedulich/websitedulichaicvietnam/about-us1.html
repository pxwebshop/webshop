About Us




AIC Vietnam is a travel agency specializing in organizing inbound and outbound tours. Through years, AIC Vietnam has affirmed its position and brand name. AIC has been successfully organizing tours for mass organizations, industrial zones and retail customers. . AIC Vietnam is highly recommended by domestic and foreign customers for the quality of service and professionalism in organizing the tours.
AIC Vietnam is providing:
- [Inbound] welcomes international tourists to Vietnam, especially Chinese and Japanese tourists.
- [Outbound] Take Vietnamese tourists to travel abroad
- [Land tour] Wholesale land tour Japan, land tour Australia.
- [Domestic] Organizing domestic tourism for industrial zones, and agencies and organizations
- [Medical Tourism] Medical tourism in Japan
- [Air ticket] Air ticket
- [M.I.C.E] Workshop and Event
AIC Vietnam is proudly to serve tours for large of organizations in Vietnam, such as:
- Vietnam Airlines - Vietnam Airline
- Bao Viet Corporation
- Euro Window Joint Stock Company.
- Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company - Vinamilk
