DA NANG - THAN TAI MOUNTAIN - HOI AN - BA NA HILL

DA NANG - 时长: 4 days 3 nights

建议的行程

  • Day 1: BAC NINH - DA NANG - MY KHE BEACH
  • Day 2: DA NANG - THAN TAI MOUNTAIN - HOI AN
  • Day 3: BA NA - DA NANG
  • Day 4: SON THA - SHOPPING - BAC NINH

(Lunch, dinner)

08:30 Car and HDV pick up at Bao Viet Life Center Bac Ninh departure to Noi Bai airport for flight 11:15 to Da Nang, to Da Nang airport bus pick up lunch.
12:30 After lunch at the hotel to rest.

In the afternoon, relax at My Khe beach.

19:00 Dinner at the restaurant. After dinner, free to explore the city: bridge, dragon bridge ...

Freedom to experience the sensation with the Sun Rays - Top 10 rounds of the world's highest, admire the beauty of Da Nang city at night lights (cost self-sufficiency). Overnight in Da Nang.

     

(Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
Have breakfast at Hotel.

07:00 car and HDV pick up you go to the hot mineral springs park.

08:00 delegation to the resort, HDV ticket to check the entrance to the gate. You participate in the following activities:
  • Swim in lazy river
  • Mineral bath at the natural mineral bath.
  • Massage chair at Ficus massage pool
  • Water games
  • Turkish sauna, Russian sauna
Visit mineral mines and tropical fruit gardens

* / Note: Participants of services not mentioned above will be charged such as: mud bath, bath onsen, bath tea, milk ... cost self

11:00 You break the hot mineral springs on the car depart for Da Nang, then lunch to the hotel to rest.

16:00 Arrive to Hoi An: Visit & shopping in Old Town: Japanese Bridge Pagoda, Sa Huynh Culture Museum, hundred years old house, Phuoc Kien Assembly House.

Dinner restaurant in Hoi An. Then back to Da Nang, free to explore Da Nang City Night: Han River Quay, Dragon Bridge, Café - Bar - Discotheque, ...

      

(Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
Breakfast at the hotel.

07:30 Depart for Ba Na - Nui Chua resort, where you discover the moment of spring - summer - autumn - winter surprise in one day. The longest cable car in the world (nearly 6,000m).

Vong Nguyet Pagoda, Linh Ung Pagoda, Shakyamuni Buddha and conquer the mountain of God at 1.487m height to enjoy the scenery of Ba Na mountain and panorama Da Nang on high. After the first cable car ride, HDV buys mountain trains for you to visit wine cellar, flower garden ...

You continue to ride the cable car to the top of the mountain to participate in new adventure games such as Love Rounds, Skiver Pilots, Fire Race, Passenger cars ...

12:00 Lunch buffet and continue to play in Ba Na.

15:00 Pick up your car to rest.

Free afternoon of My Khe Beach - one of the 06 most attractive beaches in the world.

19:00 Pick up you to dinner at the restaurant.

You free to explore Da Nang Beach at night.

      

(Breakfast, Lunch)

After breakfast at the hotel, you free swimming My Khe beach or roam freely in Da Nang.

11:30 you check out procedures. Then drive to lunch.

13:30 Pick up you visit Son Tra Peninsula (Monkey Moutain) - you will have the opportunity to enjoy the city of Da Nang from the high when the car runs around a peninsula, Visiting Spiritual Self - where the Buddha Ms. Quan The Am height 65m (highest in Vietnam). Then free shopping Central specialties.

14:30 to the airport A flight back to Hanoi at 16:00.

Go to Hanoi, the car pick you up at the original appointment. End program.
