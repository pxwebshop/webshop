HANOI - DA LAT - NHA TRANG - HANOI

价格: $ 100 P/P

HANOI - DALAT - NHA TRANG - 时长: 5 days 4 nights

建议的行程

  • DAY 1: HANOI - DALAT
  • DAY 2: DA LAT - LANGBIANG TOUR
  • DAY 3: DA LAT - NHA TRANG (140 KM)
  • DAY 4: VINPEARL LAND or HOT WATER TREATMENT
  • DAY 5: NHA TRANG - HANOI (Breakfast, Lunch)
  • Price & condition
  • 图集

      

(Lunch, dinner)
 
05h00: Pick up passengers at the meeting point to Noi Bai airport for flight VJ403 (07:15 - 09:05) to Dalat.

09h00: At Lien Khuong airport, transfer to Da Lat city (35km - about 40p). On the way, you visit Prenn Waterfall - at the foot of Prenn Pass gate to enter the city of thousand flowers. Here you can join the program to ride the elephant watching the scenery and take pictures (Tickets riding elephant 300,000 vnd / person / cost - the cost).

12h00: Group lunch at the restaurant. Then arrive at the hotel, take a rest.

14h30: You visit the Clay Tunnel, Truc Lam Meditation Center- Tuyen Lam Lake, Datanla Waterfall (sliding ticket at the self-sufficiency).

18h00: Dinner at the restaurant. After dinner, the delegation to the hotel to rest.

Evening free to explore the city of Da Lat at night: enjoy the delicious food at night market, taste mountain coffee, sightseeing Xuan Huong Lake.
Overnight at Dalat Hotel 3 * or 4 *.

 

      

(Breakfast, lunch, dinner)
 
08h00: After breakfast, you depart to visit LangBiang - a famous tourist attraction many domestic and foreign tourists come here picnic (cost self-sufficient zeep).

Langbiang mountain has two mountains, people named the two mountains are "Mount Ong and Nui Ba", 12 km from downtown Da Lat. Mount Langbiang is located at an altitude of 2.167m, is like the "roof" of Dalat, and is attractive tourist attractions of the city of Dalat. At the top of Lang Biang, there is a stele telling the story of the legendary Langbiang.

On the way, visit the Church of the Chicken, Domaine De Marie Church or Valley of Love, XQ history (cost self ticket).
12h00: Have lunch at the restaurant.

14h00: Visiting camp Linh Phuoc - one of the places that tourists often mention. The temple is also called Wat Chai Ve Chai because the temple has a 49m long dragon made of 12,000 bottles of beer.

On the delegation, visit Da Lat Train Station or City Flower Garden (cost self-ticket).

18h00: Dinner at the restaurant. You are free to roam Da Lat at night, buy jam as gifts ...
 
* You can choose alternative tours:
Cu Times Village + Strawberry Garden
Van Thanh Flower Village + Me Linh coffee + Elephant waterfall + Linh An pagoda
Overnight at Dalat Hotel 3 * or 4 *

     

(Breakfast, lunch, dinner)
 
07h00: After breakfast, you check out hotel, departure to Nha Trang (140 km, about 4 hours). The delegation stopped photographing at a height of 1.500m at the top of Hon Giao. Resting along the road at 20 'Beach.

11h00: Go to Nha Trang, you visit Long Son Pagoda, admire the statue of Buddha Mount 24m high Buddha on the hilltop.

12h00: Have lunch at the restaurant with bold flavor of Nha Trang. After lunch, you get hotel room to rest.
15h00: Transfer to the mud bath resort I-resort. Here, you can choose the service to enjoy the wonderful moments of relaxation when soaking in the hot mineral mud, swimming in the pool, waterfall, relax with Jacuzzi Jacuzzi.

18h00: Have dinner at the restaurant.

Evening, free walking street by night: visit night market, square, walk the coast ...

Overnight at Nha Trang Hotel 3 * or 4 *.

      

(Eat: optional OPT)
 
* You can choose 1 of the tours as below:

OPTION 1: VINPEARL LAND ENTERTAINMENT ENTERTAINMENT (Breakfast, Dinner)

08h00: After breakfast, car and HDV pick you up to travel and entertainment Vinpearl Land. You pass 3320m by the longest cable car system in Vietnam to KDL Vinpearl Land (cost self-ticket), where you will participate:
  • Outdoor games: Spinning sensation, cable gondola, snorkeling, roller coasters, flying bikes, pirate ships, circus animals.
  • Indoor games: 4D movies, car crash, fairy garden, children's paradise, game supermarket, karaoke room ...
  • Aquarium: With an area of ​​over 3,400 m2, is the miniature ocean with over 300 species of beautiful fish, rare and fancy.
  • Water Park: a thrilling game area, a lake, a lazy river, a family adventure zone ...
  • Water Music Theater: Modern monochrome and colorful shimmering.
17h00: You return to the coastal city.

19h00: After dinner at the restaurant. Free to roam, visit the coastal city.

Option 2: SIGHTSEEING TOURS: BATHROOM - HON MUN (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

Morning: Have breakfast at the hotel.

8h30: Car and HDV take you to port of Cau Da, embark on the program Nha Trang Bay.

09h30: Arrive Hon Mun. You are free to swim or join (about 1 hour):

- Swim, snorkel, dive or watch coral by glass bottom boat (self excluded).
- You participate in the game of strong feelings on the sea: flying parachute, water motor, diving with technical staff guide to explore the ocean under the water and take pictures underwater to memorize. . (cost self-sufficiency)

11h00: Return to Hon Tam Island, here include activities:
  • Lunch buffet BBQ seafood, fruit buffet
  • Enjoy the performance of Cham Pa dance, Russian dance
  • Join golf training, visit ancient houses.
  • Indulge in the pool and private beach of Hon Tam
14h30: Leave Hon Tam, you visit Hon Mieu - Tri Nguyen Aquarium (expense self).

16h00: Boat will take you back to Port Cau Da. Pick up the hotel to rest.

19h00: Dinner at the restaurant.

After dinner, you are free to roam the coastal city of Nha Trang.
 

Option 3: YANGBAY THAI ECOLOGICAL TOURISM AREA (Breakfast, lunch, dinner)


08h00: Pick up and depart for Yangbay waterfall

09h15: Go to Yang Bay, you visit the "Wood Spirit" - the ancient tree height of over 25 m, the original giant more than 20 people hug - one of the oldest trees in the forest Yang Bay.

Then, visit Yang Bay Tourist Park. You visit and hot mineral springs in natural rock lake between thousands of Ho-cho. Join in special folk games (self excluded). Continue to visit the wild bear reserve and participate in the game "bear" unique.

10h15: Next, the tram will take you to visit: National Museum of Ethnographic Museum; Enjoy the impressive performance of the artist and communicate with Raglay artists through: Legendary rock legend, Chapi lyrical, Traditional T 'flute, Tacung flute, Taleploi lute Amazing and rustic but "full of fire" of the Raglay people.

11h30: Have lunch at Dai Ngan restaurant with specialties Crocodile, Ostrich through unique dishes.

13h00: You continue to visit the area of ​​Yang Bay and Yang Khang: freedom of sightseeing, bath waterfall, massage water, take photo with the landscape art (Cuu Hổ Son, Cat family ...) . In particular, visitors will learn and listen to Yang Bay, an ancient instrument of the Raglay people.

15h00: Visit crocodile farm, try strong feelings through the program Crocodile Fishing (self ticket fee).

15h30: Depart for Nha Trang.

16h45: Back to hotel, rest.

19h00: Dinner at the restaurant.

After dinner, you are free to roam the coastal city of Nha Trang.

Overnight at Nha Trang Hotel 3 * or 4 *

      

Morning: Have breakfast at the hotel. After that, you check out procedures.

08h30: Car and HDV take you to visit Ponagar Tower - Tower is a symbol and the pride of the Cham people, built from the end of the seventh to the twelfth century.

 09h30: Continue to visit the Hon Chong - one of the ancient pagodas in the city of Nha Trang. Guests will have the opportunity to admire the statue of the 24-meter high Buddha Statue on the hilltop.
10h30: Visit Rock Church (also known as Mountain Church) - is one of the unique architectural style of France, located at the height of 12 m in the center of the city.

11h00: Have lunch at the restaurant. Then depart to airport for flight VN1560 (14: 35-16: 25) to Hanoi.

16h30: Pick up at Noi Bai airport on the original appointment.
