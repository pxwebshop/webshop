ABOUT US

LifeAbroad was established by experienced UK Alumni with the aim to help International students integrate into the new environment.

We understand that studying in a foreign country can be daunting and challenging, but it can also be a truly liberating and unforgettable experience. Let LifeAbroad be your friend and help you prepare for the new life without the hassle.

LifeAbroad offers a wide range of excellent services for international students: accommodation checking, airport pick-up, Ubuddy, arrival pack, and Schengen visa application for oversea student in UK

LifeAbroad is proud to be supported by Lancaster University - a top 10 university in the UK.
