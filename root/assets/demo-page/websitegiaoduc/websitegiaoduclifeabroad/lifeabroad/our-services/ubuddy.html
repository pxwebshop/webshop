UBUDDY

Our UBuddy is designed to provide international students with important information about settling into a new environment and living in the UK. This program covers all the basic information on various must-know matters for new students, as well as some useful tips.

A schedule will be created to fit with your specific requirement, including:

  • Pre-departure sessions to ensure that you are ready before flying to the UK
  • City and university tour to show you shopping area, restaurants, Asian supermarkets and essential spots
  • Introduce you to all the essential services. For example, the location to collect your BRP, hospital, private GP.
  • Using public transportation in the UK
  • How to open bank account
  • How to register with the Health Centre
  • How to apply for national insurance number

We provide 2 options for UBuddy which are 5 and 8 hours depending on your need.
